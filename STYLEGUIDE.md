# STYLEGUIDE.md — ABN Lookup Australia

## Brand
- **Name:** ABN Lookup Australia
- **Tagline:** Verify Australian Business Registrations
- **Logo:** 🔍 ABN Lookup Australia
- **Domain:** business.rollersoft.com.au

## Theme
- DaisyUI theme: `corporate`
- Primary: DaisyUI corporate blue
- Clean, professional, government-data-trustworthy feel

## Typography
- Headings: Bold, clear hierarchy
- Body: System sans-serif
- Data: Monospace for ABN numbers

## Components
- Cards for business listings
- Badges for categories/states
- Tables for dense data views
- Breadcrumbs for navigation

## Footer
- Must include "A Rollersoft project" with link to rollersoft.com.au
- Data attribution: ASIC Business Names Register, CC BY 3.0 AU

## Tone
- Professional, trustworthy
- "Verify" and "lookup" language (not "find" or "directory")
